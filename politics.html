Table 1 | Comparison of Candidates' Positions

Candidates' positions on each of 55 questions are assigned a category from 0 to 4. Category 1 represents a "yes" or full support while 4 represents a "no" or direct opposition. Category 2 represents partial or limited support and 3 represents deflection, slippery language, or similar posture. Category 0 indicates that the candidate has not taken a position.

Table 2 | Similarity of Positions Across All Questions

Each candidate's positions are compared to each other candidate's positions on all questions. The number of times both candidates take the same position on the same question is divided by the total number of questions where both candidates have taken a position (excludes zero).

Table 3 | Similarity of Positions on 12 Questions

Each candidate's positions on 12 questions (denoted by bold text in Table 1) are compared to each other candidate's positions on the same questions. The number of times both candidates take the same position on the same question (including where both candidates take no position) is divided by 12 (includes zero).
